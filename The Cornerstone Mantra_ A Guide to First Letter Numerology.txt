Preface: The Cornerstone as Your Personal Mantra


In numerology, the Cornerstone is the very first letter of your first name, exactly as it was recorded at birth 1, 2. Symbolically positioned at the "front" of your name, this letter represents your fundamental approach to life 3, 4. It provides deep insight into your essential character, revealing how you instinctively react to opportunities, obstacles, and the speed bumps of life 4, 5.
Just as a personal mantra is a powerful, affirmative phrase that reflects your core values and inner voice, your Cornerstone letter acts as the energetic "mantra" of your personality 6, 7. It dictates your primary motivations, your unique methods for initiating new endeavors, and the intrinsic attitudes you rely upon to navigate the world 4, 5.
Below are 26 chapters, one for each letter of the alphabet, exploring the unique traits, strengths, and approaches dictated by your Cornerstone mantra.
